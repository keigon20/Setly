Delete Your Account

You can delete your account and all associated data directly from within the Setly app. This permanently removes your event journal, profile, friend connections, and all data we hold about you.

How to delete your account in the app
  1. Open the Setly app and sign in
  2. Go to your Profile tab
  3. Tap the Settings icon (top right)
  4. Scroll to Danger Zone and tap Delete Account
  5. Type "Delete account" to confirm and tap Delete

Deletion is immediate and cannot be undone. Your account, events, ratings, comments, and friend data are permanently removed.

Can't access the app?

If you're unable to sign in to complete the deletion yourself, email us and we'll manually delete your account within 30 days.
